Senior Backend Engineer
Backend Solutions - London, UK

Who we are:
We provide scaleable backend infrastructure for some of the world's largest e-commerce platforms. Our focus is on high availability, consistency, and performance at scale.

Role Summary:
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our core services team. You will be responsible for designing and implementing robust, scaleable REST and GraphQL APIs. You will work with a modern tech stack centered around Node.js and Python, utilizing Postgres for relational data and Redis for caching. You will also play a key role in our migration to a service-oriented architecture on AWS.

What you will do:
- Design and implement high-performance backend services
- Optimize database queries and schema design in Postgres
- Implement caching strategies using Redis
- Build and maintain GraphQL and RESTful APIs
- Lead architectural discussions and system design
- Collaborate with the frontend team to define API contracts

Your Qualifications:
- Significant experience in Node.js or Python (Django/FastAPI)
- Deep understanding of relational databases and SQL
- Experience with NoSQL databases and in-memory caches (Redis)
- Strong proficiency with AWS (Lambda, RDS, ElastiCache, SQS)
- Understanding of microservices and event-driven architecture
- Strong experience with unit testing and TDD

The ideal candidate:
- Strong advocate for clean code and best practices
- Enjoys solving complex architectural challenges
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Benefits & Salary:
The salary for this role is £90,000 - £120,000 per annum.
- Performance-based bonuses
- Private healthcare and pension scheme
- Learning and development budget
