ML Insights: Senior Machine Learning Engineer

About ML Insights:
We are a data-driven startup focusing on predictive analytics for retail. Our mission is to provide businesses with real-time insights into consumer behavior using advanced machine learning models.

The Role:
As a Senior ML Engineer, you will be responsible for the end-to-end lifecycle of our machine learning models, from exploratory data analysis to production deployment. You will design and implement scaleable training pipelines and ensure the reliability of our inference services. 

What you'll do:
- Lead the development and deployment of predictive models
- Architect and maintain MLOps pipelines (Kubeflow, MLflow)
- Optimize model performance and inference latency
- Collaborate with data engineers to build robust data pipelines
- Conduct research on new algorithms and techniques
- Ensure data quality and model fairness

Requirements:
- Masters or PhD in Computer Science, Mathematics, or a related field
- Deep expertise in Python and ML libraries (Scikit-learn, PyTorch, TensorFlow)
- Strong understanding of statistics and probability
- Experience with SQL and massive datasets (BigQuery, Redshift)
- Hands-on experience with cloud platforms (GCP or AWS)
- Proficiency with Docker and Kubernetes for model serving

About you:
* Strong leadership skills and experience mentoring others
* Passion for staying up-to-date with the latest ML research
* Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail

Perks:
Salary: £100,000 - £140,000 per year
- Generous equity package
- Health insurance and annual bonus
- Flexible remote work environment
- Latest hardware and software tools
