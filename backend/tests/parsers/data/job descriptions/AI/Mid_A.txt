=== ABOUT TECH CORP ===

We are building the infrastructure for the next generation of artificial intelligence. Our team is focused on deploying high-performance LLMs at scale to solve complex enterprise challenges.

Role: Mid-level AI Engineer
Type: Contract

### The Role
This is a high-impact role for an experienced AI Engineer to lead the development of our retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems. You will work closely with our data engineering and product teams to integrate state-of-the-art models into our production environment. We need someone who can go beyond just training models and focus on performance, reliability, and deployment.

### Responsibilities:
- Develop and optimize RAG pipelines for large-scale data processing
- Fine-tune LLMs (Llama, Mistral) for specific domain tasks
- Implement vector database solutions using Pinecone or Milvus
- Scale AI services on Kubernetes and AWS infrastructure
- Improve model latency and throughput for real-time applications
- Collaborate on the design of our AI-as-a-Service API

### Technical Requirements:
* Solid experience as a Software Engineer or AL/ML Engineer
* Proficiency in Python and deep learning frameworks like PyTorch or JAX
* Hands-on experience with LLM orchestration tools (LangChain, LlamaIndex)
* Excellent understanding of NLP, transformers, and embedding techniques
* Experience with NoSQL databases like MongoDB or Cassandra
* Knowledge of CI/CD for ML (MLOps) is a plus

### Ideal Candidate
1. Strong background in software engineering principles (clean code, testing)
2. Thrives in a fast-paced, research-to-production environment
3. Excellent communication skills for cross-functional collaboration
