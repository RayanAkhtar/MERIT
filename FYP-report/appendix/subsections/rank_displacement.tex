\chapter{Rank Bump Chart (Study 01A)}

This appendix is the graphical representation of Table~\ref{tab:algorithmic-ablation}..
It is a bump chart presenting the ordinal rank shifts for all ten candidates across the four scoring configurations.
Note that this is not a continuous chart, but rather a discrete chart, lines are used to connect the same candidate between
configurations and do not imply intermediate ranks, nor do they imply a continuous ranking system.
