\chapter{Metric Prediction Charts (Study 13)}
\label{appendix:metric-prediction-charts}

Charts for the metric weight predictions (via TF-IDF) vs recruiter consensus (Section~\ref{subsec:study-13-metric-prediction}).
We have $N=4$ raters per job description, and $n = 27$ skill--role pairs. Summary metrics are available in Table~\ref{tab:study-13-metric-alignment}.
The methodological limitations are covered in Section~\ref{subsubsec:study-13-limitations} and
any threats to the validity of our results are highlighted in Section~\ref{sec:threats-to-validity}.
