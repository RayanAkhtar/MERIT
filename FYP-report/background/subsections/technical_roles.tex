\section{Technical Roles}

Technical roles refer to those within the technology sector that require a high degree of specialisation across domains such as fullstack development, cloud infrastructure management and machine learning.
These positions require a combination of specific technical competencies, including proficiencies in various programming languages, frameworks and tools alongside critical soft skills such as learning agility and problem solving.
The divdersity of these required skillsets often highlight the limitations of keyword-based screening methods, which often fail to capture the nuances of a candidate's actual demonstrated abilities.
Consequently, MERIT's multimodal approach aims to evaluate both technical evidence as well as soft skills to provide reliable, role-sensitive early-stage assessments.
