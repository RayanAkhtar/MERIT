The rising number of technical roles has led to a surge in the volume of applicants, making early-stage candidate screening a challenge. 
Traditional CV-based evaluations are cognitively demanding and prone to bias. 
Automated systems have been introduced to challenge these issues, however they often rely on rigid criteria, ignore alternative demonstrations of skill, and reproduce historical biases. 
To address these limitations, this project proposes a multimodal screening system, M.E.R.I.T (Multi-source Employment Ranking \& Insight Tool), hereafter referred to as MERIT, which combines CV data with verified behavioural and proficiency-based evidence from sources such as GitHub and LinkedIn.
MERIT evaluates candidates based on observable performance, mitigates bias, allows for customisable role-specific metrics and also provides a transparent scoring system with detailed reporting. 
Through the integration of multiple sources of evidence, as well as supporting data-driven decision-making, MERIT enhances the reliability, fairness and efficiency of technical recruitment.
