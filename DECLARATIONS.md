# AI Declarations

I am disclosing the use of Generative AI (GenAI) during the development of the MERIT project. GenAI played the role of a junior pair-programmer to assist in the implementation of my ideas, research, and architectural designs. 

While GenAI was leveraged to accelerate the implementation of technical scaffolding, I maintained absolute intellectual ownership over the conceptual research, mathematical derivation, and the final logic verification of the codebase.

## Areas of Implementation Support

I implemented the component hierarchy, while GenAI was then used to assist in the implementation of the Tailwind styling based on these designs.

I designed the evaluation methodology independently. GenAI was utilised to assist in the generation of synthetic candidate profiles (based on personas I defined) to support analysis of this project.

During the design of the "Glass-Box" transparency requirements, GenAI suggested the use of Shapley Values, to which I performed an independent academic review. This was to ensure this suggestion met the project's requirements before I manually integrated it into the scoring engine.

## Engineering Agency & Technical Oversight

I used GenAI as a productivity tool to reduce manual toil regarding project scaffolding, CSS utility patterns, and general file structure.

GenAI assisted in suggesting improvements for code readability. All docstrings and technical comments have been critically reviewed and edited by me to ensure they accurately represent the underlying logic.

The majority of logic-based debugging was also conducted by myself, while syntax-related debugging was often assisted by GenAI due these bugs not playing a significant role in the project's intellectual ownership.

## Critical Reflection and Lead Authorship

Throughout the project, ChatGPT (using GPT-4) and Google Antigravity (using Gemini Pro) were utilised as supporting technical tools. Any time that the AI was used to suggest code changes, there was always a manual audit of the code to ensure that it worked as intended.

I was solely responsible for the high-level design, architectural decisions, and the development of the initial MVP for MERIT (including the extraction engine and config framework). Where AI-suggestions were used, they were treated as raw drafts which were subsequently modified, hardened, and improved upon to meet the specific requirements of my research and evaluation plan.

## Final Declaration

I declare that the conceptual design, architectural framework, and evaluation methodology of the MERIT project are my own work. GenAI was utilised strictly as a supporting tool for implementation and technical scaffolding as outlined above where appropriate. I maintain full responsibility for the integrity of the codebase and confirm that all final implementations have been critically reviewed and verified to meet the high standards of an Imperial College MEng project.
