# AI Declarations

In accordance with academic integrity and honesty guidelines, I will disclose the usage of Generative AI (GenAI) throughout the development of the MERIT project. The primary use of GenAI was as a technical collaborator to critically manage and accelerate the implementation phase of this project following my initial research and design phase. Conceptual research, mathematical derivation and architectural design took place over the course of the project, however, GenAI was leveraged to speed up the translation of my research and design into functional code.

## Areas of Implementation Acceleration

- **Algorithmic Implementation** - I leveraged GenAI to assist in the implementation of the core algorithmic frameworks (Bayesian Fusion, Shapley Values, and Skill Decay) based on my own mathematical specifications. This helped me prioritise the high-level design validation and empirical evaluation phases of the project.
- **Frontend Architecture & UX** - I acknowledge my conceptualisation of the component hierarchy and user flows, from there, GenAI was then used to assist in the implementation of the React/Next.js components based on these designs.
- **Experimental Evaluation & Data** - I designed the evaluation methodology independently. GenAI was utilised to assist in the generation of data-processing scripts and synthetic candidate profiles (based on personas I defined), allowing for a more rigorous and statistically significant analysis.
- **Methodological Innovation** - During the design of the "Glass-Box" transparency requirements, GenAI suggested the use of Shapley Values. I performed an independent academic review to make sure this suggestion was suitable before I manually integrated it into the scoring engine.

## Productivity & Efficiency

- **Boilerplate** - I used GenAI to significantly reduce manual toil, specifically regarding project scaffolding, styling and general file structure.

- **Refactoring and documentation** - GenAI assisted in suggesting improvements for code readability, specifically comments and docstrings for functions.

## Critical Reflection and Quality Control

Throughout the project, ChatGPT (using GPT-4) and Google Antigravity (using Gemini 3 Flash) were utilised as technical collaborators. I also credit Tonic.ai as a tool for synthetic data generation for the purposes of my evaluation. All output has been manually checked to ensure accuracy and quality.

The use of GenAI was strictly gated by a "Human-in-the-Loop" verification process. No AI-generated logic was accepted without a manual audit:

The implementation of core mathematical and algorithmic logic was driven by pre-defined formulae and technical specifications (initially drafted in Notion plans) provided to the GenAI. This ensured the resulting code was a direct translation of my original research.

I was primarily responsible for the high-level design, architectural and engineering decisions to ensure that the code met the requirements of the evaluation and research plan.

## Final Declaration

I declare that the conceptual design, architectural framework, and evaluation methodology of the MERIT project are my original work. GenAI was utilised as a supporting tool for implementation and technical scaffolding as outlined above. I maintain full responsibility for the integrity of the codebase and confirm that all final implementations have been critically reviewed and verified to meet the high standards of an Imperial College MEng project.
